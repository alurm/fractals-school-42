Mandelbrot and Julia fractals interactive visualization

Usage:

	$ make
	$ ./fractol
	[u]sage: show usage
	[m]andelbrot: show mandelbrot
	[j]ulia [optional x part after decimal point] [optional y part after decimal point]: show julia
	$ ./fractol m # Enjoy! Controls will be printed to the terminal.
